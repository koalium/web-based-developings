Advanced Dice Physics

Realistic 3D dice simulation with accurate collision physics, angular momentum conservation, and energy dissipation. Features bounce direction based on impact point, angular velocity influence, and realistic scattering.

Controls & Physics

  • Click to throw dice with physics-based trajectory
  • Click + Drag to adjust throw direction and force
  • Spacebar to throw with random parameters
  • Physics includes: angular momentum, impulse response, friction, and energy loss
  • Dice lose 20% energy on cube collisions, 25% on ground collisions
