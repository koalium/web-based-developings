Dice Physics Simulation

Realistic dice rolling with accurate momentum, energy loss on collisions, and rich visual presentation. Dice lose 20% energy on cube collisions and 25% on ground collisions.

Controls

  • Drag to adjust throw direction
  • Adjust sliders to modify physics parameters
  • Reset to clear all dice
